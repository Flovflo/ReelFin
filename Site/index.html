Native Jellyfin Client

Apple-first playback for self-hosted libraries.

ReelFin is a native iPhone and iPad client for Jellyfin built around SwiftUI, AVFoundation, AVKit, and Apple playback frameworks. It is designed for people who want a fast, deterministic media client instead of a thin web wrapper.

Direct play first

ReelFin prefers Apple-native playback paths and only falls back to remux or transcode strategies when the media requires it.

Support that is easy to reach

Questions about playback, account setup, or beta testing can be sent to floriantaffin@gmail.com.

Library browsing

Home recommendations, search, detail pages, cast, seasons, and episode browsing are all rendered in a native interface.

Playback diagnostics

Built-in diagnostics help verify direct play, startup choices, subtitle behavior, and playback fallback decisions.

On-device persistence

Lightweight settings and metadata caching keep the app responsive while Jellyfin credentials remain in the iOS Keychain.
