ReelFin Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

ReelFin is an iPhone and iPad client for Jellyfin. This policy explains what data ReelFin processes, why it processes that data, and what choices users have.

Information ReelFin Processes

  • Your Jellyfin server URL and app preferences, such as playback quality and streaming settings.
  • Your Jellyfin session token after sign-in so the app can authenticate requests to your server.
  • Library metadata returned by your Jellyfin server, such as titles, artwork references, playback progress, cast information, and collection rows.
  • Optional crash diagnostics if Sentry is enabled in the production build by the app publisher.

How Data Is Used

  • To sign you in to your Jellyfin server.
  • To display your media library and continue-watching state.
  • To support playback, subtitle selection, diagnostics, and sync behavior.
  • To investigate crashes and stability regressions when crash reporting is enabled.

Storage and Security

Authentication tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain. App settings and lightweight session data are stored on-device. ReelFin does not sell personal data and does not use data for cross-app tracking.

Third Parties

ReelFin communicates directly with the Jellyfin server configured by the user. If crash reporting is enabled by the publisher, crash and diagnostic events may be sent to Sentry.

Children

ReelFin is not designed for children and is not marketed as a kids app.

Your Choices

You can sign out at any time, remove the app, or contact support to request help related to this policy.
